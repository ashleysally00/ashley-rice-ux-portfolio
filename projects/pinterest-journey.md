## Pinterest Journey & Discovery Flow

**Role** UX Content Designer · Content Strategist  
**Platform** Pinterest + Squarespace  
**Tools** Pinterest Creator Tools & Analytics · Squarespace Analytics

**The Challenge**  
Pinterest traffic was growing, but pins felt generic and the jump to the website felt disjointed.

**What I Did**  
- Reviewed Pinterest analytics to see which pins and boards performed best  
- Tested poetic, emotionally resonant titles against plain/keyword-first titles  
- Refined titles, descriptions, visuals, alt text, hashtags, and board structure  
- Created themed boards (Birthday Poems, Halloween Art, etc.)  
- Mapped the full journey from pin click → site visit → read/save/follow

**Outcome**  
Poetic titles consistently outperformed plain ones.  
Refined pins and boards now drive stronger, more consistent traffic and engagement to the site.
