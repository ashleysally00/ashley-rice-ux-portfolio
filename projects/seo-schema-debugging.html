The Challenge

Content was failing to appear correctly in search results due to structured data errors and crawl issues. Despite strong on-page content, indexing and visibility were inconsistent.

What I Did

  • Audited schema markup and validated structured data.
  • Identified crawl and indexing issues affecting discoverability.
  • Implemented technical fixes to improve search visibility.
  • Verified changes using Google tooling and manual testing.

Outcome

Search visibility and crawl reliability improved after schema fixes and technical cleanup. Content began appearing correctly in rich results and indexing stabilized.
